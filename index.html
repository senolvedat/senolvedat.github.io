About

Academic rigor, industrial application

Vedat Senol is a modeling expert — a mechanical and civil engineer with a Ph.D. As an independent consultant, he builds multidomain models and simulations of complex physical systems, predicting how they behave and fail before hardware is built, across control, energy, oil & gas, and dynamic systems.

His work couples structural, thermal, and control physics in a single multidomain model using Modelica and finite element methods — turning expensive, high-stakes design questions into simulations that answer them first.
